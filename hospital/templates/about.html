Our Legacy

Our History

Wau Teaching Hospital has stood as a pillar of resilience and hope since its inception. Originally established as a regional clinic, it has evolved through decades of growth to become one of South Sudan's premier medical institutions.

In the early 2000s, the facility underwent a major transformation, gaining its status as a Teaching Hospital. This transition empowered us to not only heal but to train the next generation of South Sudanese doctors, nurses, and specialists.

Today, we continue to modernize our infrastructure and expand our services, ensuring that the people of Western Bahr el Ghazal have access to life-saving care without compromise.
